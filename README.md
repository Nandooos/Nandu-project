# Zecpath - AI-Powered Job Portal & Automated Hiring Assistant

Zecpath is a fully autonomous AI-powered job portal designed to streamline and automate the hiring process.  
It leverages AI-driven resume screening, voice/video interviews, behavioral analysis, and automated scheduling to reduce recruiter workload and improve candidate experience.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview
- AI-based Applicant Tracking System (ATS)
- Automated AI voice & video calls
- Multilingual, gender-based voice selection
- Smart interview scheduling & reminders
- Behavioral & communication analysis
- Salary negotiation & offer automation
- Recruiter dashboards with analytics
- Secure compliance & consent management

---

## 🎯 Goals & Objectives
- Reduce manual CV screening
- Automate initial candidate screening
- Enable natural, multilingual AI conversations
- Improve interview attendance & conversion
- Deliver recruiter-ready insights
- Support scalable hiring across industries

---

## 👥 Target Users
- **Companies / Recruiters**: HR managers, startup founders, staffing agencies  
- **Candidates**: Freshers, experienced professionals, technical & non-technical job seekers  
- **Admins**: Platform administrators
